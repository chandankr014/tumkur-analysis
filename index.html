Overview of Tumakuru, Karnataka

  • Location: Tumkur, officially known as Tumakuru, is located in southern Karnataka.
  • Demography: Tumkur district has a population of over 26.7 lakh people.
  • Geography: The district covers an area of 10,597 km², characterized by elevated land intersected by river valleys.
  • Transportation: Tumkur is well-connected by road and rail networks, with three National Highways passing through the city.
  • Soil and Agriculture: The economy is primarily derived from agriculture, with coconut, areca nut, and paddy cultivation being significant.
  • Water and Rainfall: Tumkur receives heavy rainfall during August and September, influencing water availability for agriculture.
  • Temperature Ranges: Winters (October to March) are mild with temperatures ranging from 12°C to 34°C, while summers can be hot, reaching up to 40°C.

Jal Jeevan Mission Goals

  • Provide tap connections to all rural households for regular, long-term supply of safe drinking water
  • Ensure water quality and reduce water-borne diseases
  • Empower communities in planning, implementing, operating & maintaining systems
  • Promote groundwater recharge, conservation and reuse

Key Achievements

  • Increased tap water coverage from 3.23 Cr to 13.98 Cr households
  • 9-fold increase in connections in backward districts
  • 23.55 lakh women trained for water quality testing
  • 206 partner organizations and 99 institutions engaged
